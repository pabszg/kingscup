King's Cup

King's Cup is a drinking game where players draw cards and follow the corresponding rule. The game involves a cup in the center of the table, and the player who draws the fourth King card must drink the contents of the cup. It's a fun and social way to enjoy drinks with friends, but remember to drink responsibly.
